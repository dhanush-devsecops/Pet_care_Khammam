Common Questions

Need to Know?

What vaccinations does my puppy need?

Puppies typically need a series of vaccinations starting at 6-8 weeks. Contact us to get a personalized schedule!

How do I book a grooming session?

You can book directly via the WhatsApp button or fill out the form at the bottom of this page.

When should I deworm my pet?

Deworming schedules depend on age and lifestyle; puppies often begin at 2–3 weeks with follow-ups. Ask our vets for a tailored plan during your visit.

What should I feed my puppy?

Choose a high-quality puppy formula appropriate for the breed size. We can recommend brands and feeding amounts based on age, weight and health.

How often should I groom my pet?

Grooming frequency depends on coat type: long-haired breeds may need weekly brushing and monthly trims; short-haired breeds can be groomed less often. Ask our groomers for a plan.

Do you provide emergency care?

Yes — we offer emergency triage during clinic hours and can advise immediate next steps. For after-hours emergencies, we can recommend the nearest 24/7 facility.

Can I bring my pet without an appointment?

Walk-ins are accepted when possible, but appointments ensure timely care. Use the WhatsApp button to check current availability or book online.
